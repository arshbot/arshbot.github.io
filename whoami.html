Who

arshbot is the dev persona of an early 20s american born indian that goes by the name Harsha Goli. The name is derived from a childhood nickname that stuck after I wore home made robot costumes for halloween many times in a row, each iteration being dubbed arshbot2000, arshbot3000 etcetera.

Why

The design of this website is intentional. It's job is simple - to host a brief explanation of who I am, what I've done, and what I think. For this reason, this site does not require a frilly design, nor does it even require javascript. It's execution is simple and concise. No more, no less.
